# Insurance Claims Analytics Dashboard — PRISM Insurance Pvt. Ltd.

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze insurance claims, policy performance, and premium/coverage metrics — giving a clear, filterable view of claims status, policy activity, and financial breakdowns by category.

## 📌 Overview

This dashboard consolidates insurance policy and claims data into a single interactive view, helping stakeholders quickly assess premium collections, coverage exposure, claim volumes, and policy activity — broken down by demographics, policy type, and age group.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI** – Dashboard design & interactive visualizations
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** – Calculated measures (Premium Amount, Coverage Amount, Claim Amount, etc.)

## ✨ Key Features

- **KPI Cards** – At-a-glance totals for Premium Amount (5.98M), Coverage Amount (600.55M), and Claim Amount (16.91M)
- **Interactive Filters** – Slice data by Policy Number, Claim Number, and Customer ID
- **Gender-wise Policy Breakdown** – Quick comparison of Female vs. Male policyholders
- **No. of Claims by Claim Status** – Bar chart breakdown of Rejected, Settled, and Pending claims
- **Premium Amount by Category** – Comparison across Travel, Health, Auto, Life, and Home policies
- **Active/Inactive Policies** – Donut chart showing the proportion of active vs. inactive policies
- **Claim Amount by Age Group** – Trend view across Adult, Elder, and Young Adult segments, with tooltip drill-down
- **Policy Type Breakdown Table** – Pending, Rejected, and Settled claim amounts by policy type (Auto, Health, Home, Life, Travel), with grand totals

## 💡 Business Value

- Gives a single view of premium, coverage, and claim exposure across the entire policy book
- Highlights claim rejection/settlement patterns by policy type to support process improvements
- Enables quick identification of active vs. inactive policies for renewal or retention efforts
- Age-group and category-level breakdowns support targeted business decisions
